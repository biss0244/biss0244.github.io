About Me

I am a student in my 1st year of Interactive Media Design at Algonquin College School of Media & Design. I was drawn to this course because of the blend of art and technology. I was born and raised in the Ottawa Valley. Growing up I have always had a fascination with web design, going back to when I was in primary school and made my first website on Geocities. As well, I am very interested in the idea of building a web presence to show my art.

I try to keep myself active as much as possible. If not physically, then I try to keep at least one creative pursuit active in my life. My belief is that having a wide array of interests you actively take part in will keep you so focused, that it is hard to find time to feel bad about yourself. I have discovered that certain activities can help people overcome obstacles in their everyday life. For instance; I believe RPGs when played in a safe environment can work wonders for people suffering from social anxiety, or having issues with simple mathematics. I have many hobbies, and find myself picking new ones up often.
